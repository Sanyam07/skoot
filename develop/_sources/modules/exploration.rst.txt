.. _exploration:

=========================
The exploration submodule
=========================

The exploration submodule provides methods for exploring data at a very high
level and summarizing its characteristics. It can often be used to quickly
determine whether various pre-processing techniques will be required by
elucidating class imbalance, variable skewness, and extreme categorical arity.
